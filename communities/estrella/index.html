What is keep?

When you buy a new home, the builder pays a commission to the buyer's broker at closing. With a traditional agent, that money stays with the agent. With keep, we give back up to 2/3 of it — credited directly to you at closing.

keep retains the greater of $8,000 or 1/3 of the commission. You receive the rest. You find the home; we handle the registration, contract, upgrades, and closing.

keep is a program offered by Conflux Real Estate, LLC, a licensed Arizona real estate brokerage.
