What is keep?

keep is a licensed Arizona buyer's broker. When you buy a new home, the builder pays a commission to your broker, typically 2–3% of the purchase price. Most buyers never see that money.

We give back up to 2/3 of it, credited directly to you at closing. You find the home. We handle the contract, upgrades, and closing process from our Arizona office. You keep the money.
